1. Program Description

Program name: Ringo Restaurant SMS Notifications

Description: Ringo is an AI-powered phone agent for restaurants. When you place a pickup order, make a reservation, or cancel a reservation by calling a Ringo-powered restaurant, you may receive a transactional SMS confirmation to your mobile phone number. These messages confirm the details of your interaction — no marketing messages are ever sent.

2. Message Types and Frequency

You will receive SMS messages only in direct response to your actions:

  • One message when a pickup order is confirmed
  • One message when a reservation is confirmed
  • One message when a reservation is cancelled

Message frequency is low and triggered solely by your calls to participating restaurants. You will never receive unsolicited or recurring promotional messages.

3. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. Ringo does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier's standard rates may apply.

4. How to Opt Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time. Reply STOP to any Ringo message to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

To re-subscribe, reply START.
