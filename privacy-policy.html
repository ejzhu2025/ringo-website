Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Ringo ("we", "our", or "us") provides an AI-powered phone agent service for restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with Ringo or receive SMS messages sent on behalf of a participating restaurant.

2. Information We Collect

When you call a restaurant powered by Ringo or request an SMS confirmation, we may collect:

  • Your phone number (from caller ID or as provided during the call)
  • Your name (as provided during the call)
  • Order details (items, quantities, pickup time)
  • Reservation details (date, time, party size)
  • Call audio and transcripts for quality assurance

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Process your food order or reservation with the restaurant
  • Send you SMS confirmation messages for orders, reservations, and cancellations
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of the Ringo service
  • Provide customer support when requested

4. SMS Messaging

By providing your phone number during a call, you consent to receive transactional SMS messages related to your order or reservation from the restaurant using Ringo. These messages include:

  • Order confirmation with items and total
  • Reservation confirmation with date, time, and party size
  • Cancellation confirmation

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency depends on your interactions. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with the restaurant you contacted and the service providers necessary to operate Ringo (e.g., Twilio for SMS delivery), under strict data processing agreements.

6. No Marketing Use

Your phone number and personal information will never be used for unsolicited marketing, promotional campaigns, or sold to advertising networks. All SMS messages are strictly transactional and directly related to your interaction with the restaurant.

7. Data Retention

Call logs and order/reservation data are retained for up to 90 days to support dispute resolution and service quality, then deleted. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP
